Caleb Alexander

Caleb Alexander is an upcoming senior at Missouri State University. He studies Computer Science and Mathematics, while also enjoying other hobbies such as playing the saxophone, playing board games, hanging out with friends, and listening to music. The original idea for the "Pop Cultvre Monastery" was inspired by Caleb's international travels, most notably when visiting The Abbey of Monte Oliveto Maggiore in Tuscany, Italy, where over 600 years of work was done by Monks to carry on science, history, culture, and more throughout the centuries. Caleb hopes to havea career in Computer Science while also maintaining avenues for artistic development.
